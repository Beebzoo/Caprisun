# 200 General Knowledge Questions

Eight categories, 25 questions each. Compiled for general curiosity rather than scraped from "most asked" lists, which aren't a real dataset. Mix of easy, medium, and genuinely challenging — with some European/Dutch content mixed in alongside the international material.

Answer follows each question. If you want to self-test, cover the answer column with your hand or strip out the "A:" lines.
